# SOP-SYN-001: synthetic chilled-water startup

This procedure is test content, not an operating manual.

1. Confirm that the fictional maintenance isolation is cleared by the site process.
2. Start P-CHW-01 through the fictional plant controller.
3. Confirm continuous flow proof for 30 seconds.
4. Confirm the active primary-loop differential-pressure target is 55 kPa.
5. Only after steps 2 through 4 are satisfied may the plant controller enable CH-01.
6. Observe TT-101 and TT-102 without issuing commands from the Workbench.

The Project Copilot Workbench is read-only and must refuse requests to execute this sequence on live equipment.
